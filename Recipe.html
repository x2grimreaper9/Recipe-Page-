Authentic Butter Chicken

This butter chicken recipe is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a mildly spiced tomato-based sauce. Itâ€™s perfect to serve with naan or rice and is easy enough for beginners to make at home. A delicious combination of flavor, spice, and comfort in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (preferably thigh or breast), cut into pieces
  • 1 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (Kashmiri chili for color)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or melted butter

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Cook the Chicken
  3. Make the Gravy
  4. Combine and Simmer
  5. Garnish and Serve
